Site plan - Template

Site Name

The site name will be named Finebakes

I chose this name because it represents the objective or mission of our webpage which is to help people create delicious recipes.

Site Purpose

The site is a bakery club where anyone can join and learn how to bake desserts. The purpose is to build a community that loves to share the best recipes that worked for them. Also people can join meetings or events and that way start learning together.

Scenarios

  • Where can I share this dessert I made that my family loved?
  • How can I make a birthday cake for my best friend?
